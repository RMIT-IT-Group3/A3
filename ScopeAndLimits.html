Scope & Limits

Project Deliverables

FlickDev Group determined that our primary goal was to deliver was to develop and deliver a functioning prototype of the FlickDeck mobile app; we identified that we wanted to demonstrate the core user experience and workflow of the application. Creating this prototype was a feasible task for the FlickDev Group and was within our current skill set: we wished to convey the project aim of a simple, easy to use application through creating a functioning prototype that is accessible, fun to use and delivers content in a dynamic way.

Project Exclusions

Due to financial concerns, and a lack of prior experience and expertise, we will be unable to develop and incorporate the data scraping tools, database management software, application and website design and development and payment platforms mentioned in the project description.
