Roles

Continuing our group work approach from Assessment 2, FlickDev Group found that allowing each member to dynamically work within or contribute to different areas allowed us to create a high standard of work. With each member willing to take on a range of tasks and help other members with assigned reports, we did not feel it necessary to define specific roles while working on this project. This is in part due to our ability as a group to evenly distribute the workload and actively contribute ideas or feedback between members.

While working on various aspects of this project, such as the video presentation and prototype wireframe, we established that several group members had professional experience in or prior knowledge of similar tools or processes.
