Skills and Jobs

Overview

FlickDeck is an exciting new application that is designed to drastically improve the user experience when it comes to streaming content, by introducing a fun, simple way to determine what content couples or groups are interested in watching.

To create and develop this application, the FlickDev Group is looking to bring on a number of bright, talented and dedicated members to our team to help us realise our dream.

Position 1 – Developer

FlickDev Group are looking for a proficient Developer to help develop our exciting new FlickDeck application.

As a Developer, you will be responsible for creating, testing and deploying program code to build and modify software required for the FlickDeck applications. You will be tasked with creating prototypes of software, and testing and analysing code for bugs and to implement improvements and fixes. You will also be required to write and maintain up-to-date technical programs, end user documentation and operational procedures.

Skills:
  • Eclipse
  • Java
  • Python
  • SQL
  • Relational Database Management Systems (RDMS)
  • GIT/GitHub

Qualifications:

Bachelor of Computer Science or Bachelor of Information Technology


Experience:

We are looking for a Developer who can demonstrate an extensive knowledge of key programming languages, including Java and Python. You are expected to have experience with SQL and Relational Database Management Systems (RDMS). You will need to showcase strong mathematical, problem-solving and analytical skills to develop and maintain effective code. You will have extensive experience in working in an active team & group environment to produce quality results and solutions. Prior knowledge of version control software and collaborative tools, especially Git and GitHub, is highly recommended.

We are looking for individuals who thrive in a creative team setting and bring excellent communication, collaborative and interpersonal skills the FlickDev Group.

Position 2 – Database Administrator

FlickDev Group are looking for a detail-orientated Database Administrator to help bring our exciting new FlickDeck application to market.

As the Database Administrator, you will be tasked with assessing project requirements to design, develop, configure and maintain custom databases to suit our application’s needs. You will be required to liaise with the team to determine data needs, data flow design and database configuration, as well as be responsible for maintaining regular data backups and developing data recovery plans.

Skills:
  • SQL
  • GIT/GitHub
  • Communications Skills

Qualifications:

Bachelor of Information Technology


Experience:

We are looking for someone who is organised and can demonstrate extensive hands-on experience with data and databases. You will have strong knowledge of Relational Database Management Systems (RDMS) and SQL, as well as excellent communication skills with the ability to liaise with technical and non-technical parties. You will have extensive experience in working in an active team and group environment to produce quality results and solutions. Prior knowledge of version control software and collaborative tools, especially Git and GitHub, is highly recommended.

We are looking for individuals who thrive in a creative team setting and bring excellent communication, collaborative and interpersonal skills the FlickDev Group.

Position 3 – UX/UI Designer

FlickDev Group are looking for a passionate UX/UI Designer to help bring our FlickDeck application to life.

As the UX designer, you will be responsible for creating and developing designs and prototypes for your mobile and web-based applications, with a focus on simplicity, accessibility and user-friendliness. You will also be tasked with researching and analysing the user experience to help inform changes or modifications to the application.

  • Figma / Sketch / XD
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Git/GitHub
  • Communications Skills
  • Presentation Skills

Qualifications:

Bachelor of Design or Bachelor of Information Technology


Experience:

We are looking for designers that can demonstrate a commitment to exceptional visual design, with an eye for detail and passion in creating designs. You will need to demonstrate a proficiency in creating and managing user research and testing programs, with the ability or craft innovative solutions. You will showcase an extensive experience with wireframing and prototyping tools such as Figma, and a solid understanding of programming languages like HTML, CSS and JavaScript. You will be tasked with creating, programming, testing and maintaining apps, and work alongside other teams to identify and solve development challenges.
